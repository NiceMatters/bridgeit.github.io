Community

BridgeIt fosters a vibrant and open community, and it's completely free to use. The BridgeIt JavaScript API is open sourced, and available on GitHub. The BridgeIt native applications are also free, and available on all the major mobile app stores (platform icons with links). Join the BridgeIt community and get access to forums, blogs, and so much more. BridgeIt welcomes you with open arms.

Need Help?

BridgeIt is as easy as it gets. Simply add bridgeit.js to your HTML5 page and you are off and running. The Getting Started Guide shows you how to add native capabilities to your HTML5 application in less than 5 minutes. Need more help?
